The third edition of the NLP4KGC workshop, held in 2024, continued the success of our workshop series with contributions from researchers worldwide.

This edition featured keynote presentations, peer-reviewed papers on entity recognition, relationship extraction, and knowledge graph applications, as well as in-depth discussions on emerging trends in NLP-based knowledge construction.

1st NLP4KGC - 2022

The inaugural NLP4KGC workshop brought together the community to explore the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Knowledge Graph Construction.

This first edition established the foundation for what would become a successful and recurring workshop series, featuring pioneering work on neural entity recognition, dependency parsing for KG construction, and knowledge base population techniques.

About the NLP4KGC Series

The NLP4KGC workshop series has established itself as a premier venue for researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Knowledge Graph Construction. Each edition brings together the community to share research, discuss challenges, and explore new directions in the field.

With growing participation and increasing recognition, the workshop continues to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among academic institutions and industry organizations worldwide.
