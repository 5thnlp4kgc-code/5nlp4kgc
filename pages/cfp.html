Submission Guidelines

Research Topics

We invite submissions on, but not limited to, the following topics:

  • Entity Recognition and Linking from Text
  • Relation Extraction and Semantic Role Labeling
  • Event Extraction and Temporal Reasoning
  • Coreference Resolution and Entity Disambiguation
  • Knowledge Graph Completion and Link Prediction
  • Knowledge Base Construction and Maintenance
  • Neural Methods for Knowledge Graphs
  • Knowledge Graphs in Information Extraction
  • Knowledge Graph Applications and Evaluation
  • Multilingual and Cross-Lingual Knowledge Graphs
Submission Format
  • Paper Length: 4-8 pages for regular papers, 2-4 pages for position papers
  • Format: PDF only
  • Language: English
  • Citation Style: ACL proceedings format recommended
  • Anonymity: Blind review (remove author information)
Submission System

All submissions must be made through EasyChair

Please ensure your paper is properly anonymized and follows the submission guidelines before uploading.

Review Process

Papers will be reviewed by at least 2 members of the program committee. The review process will follow standard double-blind peer review principles.

Acceptance decisions are based on:

  • Technical soundness and novelty
  • Relevance to workshop topics
  • Clarity of presentation
  • Significance of contributions

Questions?

For inquiries about the call for papers, please contact the workshop organizers.
